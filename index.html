❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Crystal faster than Ruby?

Yes. Crystal is compiled and typically much faster than interpreted Ruby.

Does Crystal support concurrency?

Yes. Crystal uses lightweight fibers for concurrent execution.

What is type inference in Crystal?

Crystal automatically infers types at compile time, reducing the need for explicit declarations.

Is Crystal production ready?

Crystal is evolving and suitable for many use cases, but its ecosystem is still growing.
